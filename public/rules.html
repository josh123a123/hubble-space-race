Hubble Space Race

Rules

  1. Spin the wheel to see how many lightyears you'll fly through space
  2. Answer the questions to earn resources
  3. After you answer the questions, if no one else has claimed it you can choose to build a Research Station on the celestial body. If someone has claimed it, you will have to pay them for letting you stop there.
  4. Keep spinning, answering questions, and building Research Stations. Once you've reached 500 space $$ you will have conquered space!

Your Resources

Hydrogen = 1

Boron = 5

Neon = 10

Calcuium = 20
