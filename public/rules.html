Hubble Space Race

Rules

  1. Spin the wheel to see how many light-years you'll fly through space
  2. Answer the questions to earn resources
  3. After you answer the questions, if no one else has claimed it you can choose to build a Research Station on the celestial body. If someone has claimed it, you will have to pay them for letting you stop there.
  4. Keep spinning, answering questions, and building Research Stations. Once you've reached a total of 500 resources you will have conquered space!

WATCH OUT: The bonus rounds will stop game play for all players. The first player to answer the questions right will get the bonus resources.

HINT: The information at each stop will show up again in the bonus rounds.
